А тепер трохи усної практики


1. Do you know any Christmas carols? Can you sing one? Is there any Christmas song that you find annoying?
2. Did you believe in Santa Claus when you were a child? Did you use to write a letter to Santa when you were younger? What kinds of things did you ask for?
3. Do you remember last Christmas? What was the most enjoyable part? What was the least enjoyable moment? What will you do differently this year?
4. Do you attend any Christmas events?
5. What makes Christmas time special in your country? How do you describe Christmas atmosphere?
6. Do you know where live Santa? Can you name three countries in this area?
7. Have you ever eaten one of traditional English dishes? If, yes how they were. If no, what kids of them would you like to try.
8. Do you prepare your house or flat to Christmas?
9. What dishes do you cook to Christmas dinner?
