Завдання на Must / Mustn't

Exercise 1. Джек приїхав в літній табір. Вожата пояснює йому правила поведінки. Заповни пропуски дієсловами, які будуть підходити по сенсу:


  • Can
  • Must
  • Mustn’t

1. You _____ play football there.
2. You _____ brush your teeth every day.
3. You _____ leave the camp.
4. You _____ play on our sports grounds.
5. You _____ go to bed at nine o'clock.
6. You _____ walk at night alone.
7. You _____ fight with other children.
8. You _____ buy sweets in our shop.
9. You _____ watch TV in the evening.
10. You _____ swim in our swimming pool.
11. You _____ make your bed every morning.
12. You _____ call your parents every evening.
13. You _____ get up at eight in the morning.

Answers 1

1. Can
2. Must
3. Mustn’t
4. Can
5. Must
6. Mustn’t
7. Mustn’t
8. Can
9. Can
10. Can
11. Must
12. Must
13. Must

Exercise 2. З’єднай речення з попереджувальними знаками.


1. You mustn’t touch this. ___
2. You mustn’t swim there at all. ___
3. You must throw away your wastes. ___
4. You mustn’t come in with animals. ___
5. You must go slowly. ___
6. You must drive slowly. ___
7. You mustn’t come so close to cage. ___


Answers 2

1 - 4; 2 - 6; 3 - 2; 4 - 3; 5 - 5; 6 - 7; 7 - 1.

Exercise 3 Напиши які з цих дій діти можуть робити, а які ні.


Наприклад: 0. (watch films a lot) – Children mustn’t watch films a lot.


1. _________________________ (be polite)
2. _________________________ (be rude)
3. _________________________ (fight)
4. _________________________ (shout in class)
5. _________________________ (be tidy)
6. _________________________ (wear shorts to school)
7. _________________________ (push each other)
8. _________________________ (do homework)
9. _________________________ (go to school)
10. _________________________ (help their parents)

Answers 3

1. Children must be polite.
2. Children mustn’t be rude.
3. Children mustn’t fight.
4. Children mustn’t shout in class.
5. Children must be tidy.
6. Children mustn’t wear shorts to school.
7. Children mustn’t push each other.
8. Children must do homework.
9. Children must go to school.
10. Children must help their parents.
