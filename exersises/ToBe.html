Exercise 1. Зроби по прикладу.


Приклад: (він) - He is kind

1. (вони) _________________ funny.
2. (він) _________________ big.
3. (воно) _________________ blue.
4. (я) _________________ happy.
5. (вона)_________________ sad.
6. (ми) _________________ boys.
7. (вони)_________________ small.
8. (воно)_________________ yellow.
9. (ти)_________________ a girl.

Answers 1

1. They are funny.
2. He is big.
3. It is blue.
4. I am happy.
5. She is sad.
6. We are boys.
7. They are small.
8. It is yellow.
9. You are a girl.
